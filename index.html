About

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Tongji University, advised by Prof. Jihong Guan. My research focuses on causal discovery and medical AI, with particular interest in conditional independence testing, causal representation learning, and biomedical vision-language models.

Before joining Tongji University, I studied in the Department of Mathematics at Shantou University under the supervision of Prof. Zhifeng Hao, and received joint training at the Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Associate Prof. Hao Zhang. I received my undergraduate degree from China University of Mining and Technology.

I am open to research discussions, collaboration, and careful paper-level feedback. Please feel free to contact me.
